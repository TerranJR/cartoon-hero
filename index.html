About Captain Jack

Captain Jack Sparrow is a notoriously fierce, and drunken pirate from the Caribbean. He is known for his quirky jokes and grand adventures. He sails the 7 seas in search of treasure as all pirates do, but he meets some mysterious and dangerous people along the way.
